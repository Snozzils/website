Careful Crossing

A gentle picture book about stopping, thinking, and crossing safely.

Ages 3–6 Picture Book Everyday Safety

In Careful Crossing, Esme and Bailey learn that even familiar roads need calm thinking and careful choices. This warm, child-friendly story introduces simple road awareness in a reassuring way.

Many parents look for simple ways to introduce road awareness to young children. Careful Crossing provides a calm and reassuring story that helps children aged 3–6 understand why stopping, looking, and listening matters when crossing roads.
