Well suited to early years story time

The Adventures of Esme & Bailey uses familiar childhood situations, gentle language, and warm storytelling to support calm reading experiences and thoughtful conversation.

The books work well for shared reading, simple discussion, and helping young children talk about what characters notice, feel, and choose.
