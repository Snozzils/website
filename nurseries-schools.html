Early Years

For Nurseries & Schools

The Adventures of Esme & Bailey is a picture book series designed around familiar childhood moments, making it well suited to story time, shared reading, and gentle discussion.

Why the series works in early years settings

The stories use simple, relatable situations and child-friendly language to support calm reading experiences and thoughtful conversation.

They are ideal for reading together and talking about what characters notice, feel, and choose.

Best used for

  • Story time
  • Shared reading
  • Gentle discussion
  • Everyday awareness
  • Warm conversations with young children

Themes explored in the series

Clear, simple themes that fit naturally into early years conversation.

Kindness
Everyday awareness
Calm thinking
Safety
Listening
Teamwork
Making good choices
Confidence
