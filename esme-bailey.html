Meet the Characters

Meet Esme & Bailey

Esme and Bailey are at the heart of every adventure — a warm, thoughtful pair who face little everyday challenges together.

Meet Esme

Esme is curious, kind, and still learning about the world around her. Through each story, she meets new situations, makes choices, and grows a little more along the way.

Meet Bailey

Bailey is a playful and loyal companion with a gentle nature. Sometimes silly, sometimes thoughtful, he is always by Esme’s side as they explore each new adventure together.

Together in every story

A team through every story

Together, Esme and Bailey experience the small moments that matter in childhood — learning, pausing, noticing, and helping one another through everyday adventures.

The series is built around warmth, teamwork, and gentle lessons children can understand.
